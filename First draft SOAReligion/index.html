IN ATHENS

Settled among the predominantly Protestant hills of Appalachia and home to Ohio University, Athens, Ohio stands out as a hub for education, community, and diversity. Since the town was founded in 1805, many religious and spiritual communities have emerged, each establishing their own formal and informal practice locations.
