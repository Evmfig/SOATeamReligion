IN ATHENS

Settled among the predominantly Protestant hills of Appalachia and home to Ohio University, Athens, Ohio stands out as a hub for education, community and diversity. Since the town's founding in 1805, it has often been called a "bubble" of inclusivity, and because of this many religious and spiritual communities have emerged here, with each establishing their own formal and informal practice locations.
